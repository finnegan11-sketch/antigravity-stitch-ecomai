Background & Narrative

Building the Agentic Era of Global Commerce.

The shift from search-based shopping to discovery-led retail is complete. Now, we enter the era where AI doesn't just suggest—it executes.

The Journey

With over a decade of experience across global marketplaces—from hyper-growth startups to legacy giants—I've witnessed three major evolutions in retail. First, the move to digital. Second, the shift to social and discovery.

Today, we are at the precipice of the third: Agentic Commerce. This is the transition from a catalog of items to a network of autonomous agents that understand intent, negotiate value, and manage logistics on behalf of the consumer.

My mission at ecomai.ai is to architect the strategy for this transition. I bridge the gap between complex LLM capabilities and the practical realities of SKU-level inventory, margin optimization, and customer trust.
